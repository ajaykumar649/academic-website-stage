    <h3>Principal Investigator</h3>
    <p>Welcome to our Experimental High Energy Physics group at the Institute of Science, Banaras Hindu University. Our laboratory is actively engaged in pushing the frontiers of particle tracking hardware and detector technology alongside heavy-ion phenomenological modeling. </p>
    <p>Our core strengths focus heavily on simulation-based performance optimization of gaseous Gas Electron Multiplier (GEM) tracking stations and radiation-hard silicon Low-Gain Avalanche Detectors (LGADs) engineered for upcoming high-luminosity experiments.</p>
