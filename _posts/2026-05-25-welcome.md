Our research group at the Department of Physics, Banaras Hindu University, is delighted to share recent milestones as we expand our instrumentation and phenomenological research tracks. 

We are officially welcoming our doctoral research scholars to their respective core tracking vectors:
* **Rajiv Gupta** is driving our detector simulation frameworks, optimizing multi-gap Gas Electron Multiplier (GEM) tracking stations and low-gain avalanche silicon sensor geometries.
* **Sunidhi Saxena** is expanding our phenomenological models tracking heavy hadron production dynamics across high-energy center-of-mass collision matrices.

Stay tuned for upcoming pre-prints, simulation pipeline software disclosures, and detector instrumentation deployment logs!
